Green Harvest Network

Connecting farmers directly with retail buyers for fresher produce and fair prices

How It Works

Farmer Register

Farmers register with their produce information and harvest seasons to connect with retailers

Store Selection

Farmers choose the desired store in which they want to sell their produce in their desired location

Create Selling Request & Transport

Farmers Create Selling Request after choosing the desired store and select the desired transport means

Direct Connection

Retailers review and accept requests, connecting directly with farmers for negotiations
